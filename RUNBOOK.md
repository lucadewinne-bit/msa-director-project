# Staff Runbook — MSA Script Builder (1 page)

A staff member can run this **without Luca in the room**. Print this page or keep it open.

**What it is:** a tool that helps you and the kids turn a movie idea into a filmed short.
**You** (staff) drive it; the **kids** decide the story; they never chat with the AI themselves.
**Always used with staff present. No child names, photos, or recordings — ever.**

**Open it:** **https://msa-director-project.vercel.app** on a laptop or tablet. No login needed.
Turn the **volume up** — it reads things aloud for the kids.

---

## Run a session (about 20–30 minutes)

1. **New movie** → a quick setup asks the kids' **age** (and, if you like, length + how many
   actors). Fill it in and press **Start**. (This makes the story fit the kids in the room.)
2. **💡 Brainstorm** — a kid pitches an idea. You type it in. Read Claude's questions **out loud**;
   the kids answer; you type their answers back. Keep going until the story is ready.
3. **📝 Script** (optional) — press **Write the script** to see the whole movie written out.
   You can **edit** the words right there, press **Hear it 🔊**, and **Save 💾**.
4. **🗂️ Step cards** — press **Step cards**. Then **check them** with the kids: fix any words,
   press **Skip** on ones you don't want (they wait safely in the **Stash**), then **Show the kids**.
5. **🎥 Film** — in the studio, one card at a time. Press **🔊 Hear it** to read a card aloud.
   Use MSA's normal cameras, green screen, and stop-motion. **🎬 Storyboard** shows all the shots at a glance.

## Save, reopen, and move a movie
- **Save 💾** and give the movie a name → it appears under **Your movies** on the home screen.
- Each movie shows what's inside (📄 Script · 🗂️ cards · 🎬 Storyboard). Tap the movie to open it,
  or tap one part to jump straight to it.
- **⬇️ Back up** downloads a file of all your movies; **⬆️ Restore** loads it (e.g. on another computer).

## Getting around
Every full screen has **🏠 Home** on the **left** (→ Your movies) and **✕ Close** on the **right** (→ back one step).

---

## If something looks wrong
- **Says "PRACTICE MODE"** → the AI isn't connected. Tell Luca (the key setting needs fixing).
- **"Couldn't reach the server"** → check the internet and refresh the page.
- **A kid's name comes up** → gently steer back: *"Let's describe the story, not the kids."*

## The one rule
**Describe the story, not the kids.** No names, ages, schools, photos, or recordings — the app
never stores anything about a child.
