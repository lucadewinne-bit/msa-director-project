# VISION.md — The North Star (STRICTLY after July 17)

## The idea
An open-source online platform for kid-friendly video production that uses AI
the way MSA uses green screens: as a magic trick that AMPLIFIES the human part
instead of replacing it. AI helps kids plan, storyboard, and produce ambitious
projects — but the heart of it stays physical and together: paper, scissors,
color pencils, kids shoulder to shoulder, performing, negotiating, laughing.

## The point of view (this is the differentiated part)
In an AI-dominated world, the scarce skills are the human ones — creativity,
empathy, collaboration, telling a story to a room. Media production is one of
the best trainers of those skills, and programs like MSA prove it. The platform's
thesis: AI should lower the executive-functioning and technical barriers to
making things, so kids spend MORE time on the human parts, not less. Built with
accessibility (dyslexia, ADHD, pre-readers) as a first-class design constraint,
because the founder lived it.
