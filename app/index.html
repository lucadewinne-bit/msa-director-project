👋 Welcome — what is this?

The Script Builder is for staff and kids to use together, always with a grown-up. You type and read the questions out loud — the kids decide the story. The kids never chat with the computer themselves.

Here is the whole path from idea to movie:

💡 Brainstorm the story → 📝 Build & lock the script → 🗂️ Make the step cards → 🎥 Film in the studio

The first three steps happen here in the app. Filming happens in your real studio. Need a hand? Open help any time.

❓ Help

Quick answers. Tap 🔊 on a kid answer to hear it read out loud.

For the staff member

What is this app?
It helps you and the kids turn a movie idea into filming steps. You type and read aloud; the kids decide the story. Kids never chat with the computer.
How do I start?
On the home screen pick a starter idea or "Blank story", then type the kids' idea and press Send 🎥.
How do I change the story or script?
Just keep chatting. Type what the kids want different and the co-writer updates the story — as many times as you like.
How do I make the step cards?
When the story is ready, a glowing "Step cards 🗂️" button appears. Press it and the cards are made for you.
How do I fix or skip a card?
On the check screen, type right on a card to fix any words. Press "Skip" to send a card to the Stash — nothing is ever deleted, and one tap brings it back.
What if a kid gets stuck?
Read the last question again, offer two choices, or press "Hear it 🔊". There's no wrong answer — keep it playful.
Is anything about the kids saved?
No. No names, photos, or recordings — ever. Saved movies hold only the story, and only on this computer.

For the kids 🎬

What are we making?
A real movie, from your idea! 🎬
What happens next?
First we make the story. Then we make the cards. Then we film! 🎥
Whose turn is it?
Everyone helps! The director picks, and we all build the movie together. 🌟
Do I have to read?
Nope! Press the 🔊 button and it reads to you.
