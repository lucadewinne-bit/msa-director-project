Source: Claude Corps Fellow application (Greenhouse, job 4250200009) + Anthropic
Claude Corps pages. Deadline for Cohort 1 consideration: **July 17, 2026**
(rolling review — submitting earlier is better). Cohort 1 starts October 19, 2026.

## Hard requirements (application fails without these)
1. **AI Fluency course — completed, with proof.**
2. **Claude 101 course — completed, with proof.**
   → Both certificates combined into ONE document for upload.
3. **Resume/CV uploaded.**
4. **LinkedIn profile URL** (required field).
5. Eligibility: 18+, under two years of full-time professional experience,
   US work authorization not dependent on employer sponsorship.

## Written questions (~350 words each) — draft from real material only
- A time you made an impact in your community.
  → Spine: the full-circle MSA story + shipping the tool there (see CONTEXT.md).
- A time you learned from a mistake or setback.
  → Candidates: a real moment from the build (DECISIONS.md will surface one),
    or a real story from swimming / MSA productions. Honest > impressive.

## What they say they're looking for (quote-level fidelity, paraphrased)
- Built something end-to-end on your own — scoped, shipped, can talk about it
  honestly, including what you'd do differently.
- Genuine demonstrated pull toward social-impact work.
- Takes initiative without a PM; stays coachable.
- Can explain something technical to a non-technical room.
- Energized, not deflated, by ambiguity and resource constraints.
- Fellowship job shape: Discover & scope → Build with Claude → Enable the
  organization → leave something that keeps running.
  → **The project must be a miniature of this exact loop, ending with a
    one-page staff guide (the "runbook") and a real handoff at MSA.**

## Non-negotiable project constraints
1. **Child safety:** tool used WITH staff/parent, never unsupervised chat for young
   kids; zero personal data from children (no names, accounts, stored conversations).
   Testing = observing kids use it alongside staff. Nothing more.
2. **Luca builds it.** Claude teaches and drafts; Luca owns and can explain every
   part. Engineers advise/review only.
3. **Shipped beats ambitious.** V1 small enough to finish, demo in 60 seconds,
   and hand off. Anything that threatens the July 17 date gets cut or moved to
   VISION.md.
